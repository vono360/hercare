What We Offer

Comprehensive care for every chapter

Your health is more than a single concern. We provide expert, evidence-based care tailored to every stage of a woman's life.

01
Perimenopause & Menopause Care

Personalized support through every stage of hormonal transition — hot flashes, sleep disruption, mood changes, and beyond.

02
Hormone Therapy & Symptom Management

Individualized hormone therapy and non-hormonal options tailored to your goals, history, and safety profile.

03
Longevity & Preventive Health

Proactive risk assessment, lifestyle strategies, and evidence-based screening to support healthy aging.

04
Sexual Health

Compassionate, confidential care for libido, intimacy discomfort, vaginal dryness, infections, and sexual wellness.

05
Mental Health & Mood

Support for mood changes, anxiety, depression, and emotional well-being — especially during hormonal transitions.

06
Contraception

Comprehensive counseling and access to the full range of birth control options aligned with your lifestyle and goals.

07
Cervical Cancer Screening

Routine Pap tests and HPV screening with guideline-based care and timely follow-up when needed.

08
Cardiovascular Health

Heart health assessment, cholesterol and blood pressure management tailored to women's changing physiology.

09
Weight & Metabolic Management

Sustainable, science-backed strategies for metabolic health, insulin resistance, and long-term weight wellness.

10
Abnormal Bleeding Evaluation

Thorough evaluation of irregular, heavy, or unexpected bleeding with clear, effective treatment options.

11
Breast Health

Clinical breast exams, imaging referrals, and evaluation focused on early detection and lifelong breast health.

12
Urinary Tract & Pelvic Floor

Assessment and treatment for urinary symptoms, incontinence, and pelvic floor concerns to improve daily comfort.

About Dr. Alexander
"The best care happens when women feel truly heard, respected, and equipped to lead their healthiest lives."

Dr. Teressa (Terri) Alexander is a board-certified obstetrician and gynecologist with more than two decades of dedicated practice championing women's health at every stage of life.

Her approach combines rigorous evidence-based medicine with genuine warmth — she is known for her exceptional bedside manner, her willingness to truly listen, and her proactive, thorough approach to care.

HerCare Gynecology is the culmination of Dr. Alexander's vision: a practice built entirely around women — their hormones, their whole health, and every season of their lives.

  • Board-Certified Obstetrician & Gynecologist
  • Over 24 years of clinical experience
  • Specialization in perimenopause, menopause & hormonal health
  • Integrative & whole-person wellness approach
  • Serving the greater Chicago area — Schaumburg & surrounding communities
